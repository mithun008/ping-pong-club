About the Club

Where the
Game Gets Serious.

Seattle Table Tennis Club started as a small community gathering and grew into the largest dedicated table tennis club in the Pacific Northwest. We're passionate about the sport at every level.

Our state-of-the-art facility features 12 competition-grade tables, a pro shop, and dedicated training zones. We host weekly leagues, monthly open tournaments, and regular social nights.
